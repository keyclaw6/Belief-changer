## Context

The factory needs deep, source-grounded research without leaking calibration
references into generation. The first implementation made the file contract
executable, but then prescribed a four-track scout→retrieval→specialist→lead
workflow, atomic pre-collection matrices, and numeric floors. Twenty-eight
bootstrap pilots produced no accepted source and no chapter. The schemas had
become a research planner rather than a record of research.

The founder's quality-only correction is that intelligent models own the
research method. Deterministic machinery may transport contexts and measure
finished artifacts, but prompts and schemas must not dictate how a lead thinks,
searches, delegates, or decides that another specialist is needed.

## Goals / Non-Goals

**Goals:**

- Produce deep lived-experience, scientific, and investigative evidence through
  a top-reasoning lead and fresh subagents it organizes itself.
- Preserve blindness, source rights/privacy, quote fidelity, and traceability.
- Fill all ten research banks across every materially distinct reader persona.
- Keep model calls and source decisions reconstructable without duplicating
  orchestration metadata in every bank row.
- Start real research inside run-001 and reach chapter prose quickly.

**Non-Goals:**

- Prescribing a universal research org chart, search order, persona count,
  assignment matrix, or numeric stopping quota.
- Building a crawler, database, external evidence store, or agent framework.
- Retaining deletion-sensitive/nonredistributable community data.
- Changing the three-input anti-repetition chapter contract.

## Decisions

### 1. Let the research lead design the research

A fresh allowed lead receives only the research prompt and blind brief. It must
use multiple fresh-context subagents for independent depth, but chooses their
roles, number, assignments, tools, search paths, recursion, and synthesis order.
It may reorganize the work whenever evidence changes its view. The caller
provides transport and persists visible artifacts; it does not render a matrix
or enforce a scout→worker→specialist chain.

H-010 remains the prompt-structured arm because files are the handoff boundary,
not because the prompt dictates an organization. H-011 remains rejected until a
same-objective trial proves that an OSS framework improves research quality or
reliability without replacing model judgment.

### 2. Make rights and privacy an evidence gate, not a policy project

Discovery may inspect ordinary public metadata and source policies. Before
content becomes evidence, the lead records the access, excerpt, retention,
redistribution, attribution, and privacy basis. Git stores only the minimum
necessary permitted excerpt and locator—never full community posts, bulk user
dumps, private identity mappings, or deletion-sensitive/nonredistributable user
content. Reddit remains excluded without explicit Reddit authorization.

Run-001 adds no external store. A source needing deletion-aware storage simply
does not count. This is the smallest contract compatible with the repository's
CC-BY-SA content scope; a real external-store use case may justify a later
design.

### 3. Keep provenance lean and source-centered

Each accepted URL has one packet containing source identity, retrieval date,
rights/privacy basis, attribution, minimum excerpt, locator, and evidence items
tagged by bank/persona. The research log records model calls, meaningful source
decisions, rejections, and runtime metadata once. It does not repeat model,
query, and allowance fields in every persona-bank cell.

Exact quotes remain character-for-character gates. Interpretations remain
unquoted and source-linked. Search summaries guide discovery but do not become
evidence merely because a model saw them.

### 4. Use model judgment for sufficiency

The ten banks remain the output ontology. The lead chooses working targets after
seeing the domain and reports final counts and gaps, but fixed quotas do not
decide completion. A bank passes only when it contains specific, nonredundant
material strong enough to change the relevant belief for every materially
distinct persona. Volume cannot compensate for generic evidence.

### 5. Review the completed research once, independently

After packets and both syntheses exist, one fresh allowed top-reasoning reviewer
receives the complete blind evidence set. It judges depth, provenance,
rights/privacy, persona/bank coverage, scientific rigor, and usefulness for
belief change. It may accept, regenerate, or commission targeted follow-up.
There is no mandatory pre-dispatch architecture review; the lead may request
midstream critics whenever useful.

### 6. Use the greatest allowance the live call can actually receive

Every research role uses top reasoning. The caller requests the endpoint maximum
when accepted; if the endpoint/key returns a lower exact authorization ceiling,
the caller uses that ceiling rather than inventing a smaller cap or stopping the
program. A `length` finish triggers continuation. Cost, speed, latency, and token
use remain descriptive and never narrow the objective or select a winner.

### 7. End bootstrap and make run-001 real

RP-000–RP-027 are a completed failure autopsy of the over-prescribed protocol,
not a prerequisite to satisfy. The boundary proves model connectivity,
blindness, top-reasoning routing, and provenance failure detection. Actual source
collection and synthesis now occur in immutable run-001. The bird's-eye audit is
repeated after synthesis, after a fit plan, and after the first Stage-A judgment.

### 8. Keep Opus baseline first; add Muse as a later writer arm

Run-001 remains Opus 4.6 with reasoning disabled. Muse Spark 1.1 is not on
OpenRouter at the recorded preflight and requires a separate Meta credential;
its reasoning cannot be disabled. Once an official route is available, test it
after the baseline on identical plan, prompt, inputs, reviewers, and judges at
its highest reasoning mode. Report it as an end-to-end writer comparison, not a
causal model-only A/B.

### 9. Preserve blindness and downstream contracts

Research, framing, planning, review, and writing never receive reference text,
`analysis/`, or judge outputs. The style guide is the only reference-derived
carrier. Research still emits `research/sources/`, `research/research-log.md`,
`research/lived-experience.md`, and `research/scientific-evidence.md`; downstream
exact inputs and the chapter anti-repetition law remain unchanged.

## Risks / Trade-offs

- **A lead stops too early** → the independent full-evidence reviewer commissions
  deeper work; add another model critic rather than a deterministic quota.
- **Agent freedom produces messy records** → normalize only the final evidence
  packet/log fields; never normalize the reasoning path.
- **Rights-safe sources are less vivid** → search more broadly for licensed or
  consented first-person evidence; never lower the depth bar or retain forbidden
  data.
- **Muse route remains unavailable** → record the runtime miss and continue the
  Opus baseline; the experiment does not block Stage A.
