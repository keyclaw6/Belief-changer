# redesign-book-factory

## Why

The whole-factory review found that the current loop protects originality,
evidence honesty, safety, and the no-willpower method, but does not reliably
produce a completed belief transition in each chapter or a cumulative Easyway
reader journey. The dominant causes are upstream: reader-state planning,
research-to-writer transport, blocking review ownership, evaluation alignment,
and non-atomic experiment governance. Continuing the one-asset amendment loop
would test symptoms rather than those causes.

This change is based on the review titled *Whole-Factory Review — Diagnosis,
Redesign, and Next Experiments* (source SHA-256
`2e2d3b4ce2d7f81ffbe902a52a7771616b4ee9d7e0c7154a9eaf52d6ef042ade`).
Its conclusions are hypotheses to test where the report says they are, not
facts merely because they appear in the report.

## What Changes

- Replace coverage-first chapter planning with an enforceable reader-state
  contract from subject brief through plan review.
- Carry assigned source texture through audited chapter commissions while
  shrinking the writer's runtime context.
- Split grounded integrity review from whole-opening developmental review and
  route defects to their actual stage owner.
- Install a fail-closed readiness guard in `PROGRAM.md` and every legacy
  product-generation/promotion entrypoint as the first implementation task, so
  the retired loop cannot reach models or writes while redesign is incomplete.
- Replace the one-edit/one-score campaign with bounded causal-bundle
  experiments, isolated candidate products, atomic promotion, and separate
  integrity, reader-effect, sequence, and reference-sighted craft evidence.
- Freeze H-F01's immutable causal authority before RF-21, then validate the
  treatment opening through three position-swapped chapter comparisons and one
  position-swapped whole-opening comparison against exact offset-matched GSBS.
  Keep fresh control/treatment observations as a separate causal diagnostic.
  Preserve failed RF-20 as terminal; neither it nor its unapproved successor is
  a prerequisite.
- Preserve the founder ladder after Stage A: complete quit-sugar parity is Stage
  B, then untouched caffeine with zero tuning is Stage C.

## Capabilities

### Modified Capabilities

- `book-pipeline` — changes the brief, synthesis, framing, plan, commission,
  writer-context, review, and revision contracts while preserving the stable
  production-book folder and core artifact sequence.

### New Capabilities

- `factory-experimentation` — specifies causal experiments, independent
  product evaluation, candidate isolation, atomic promotion, and the evidence
  required before claiming cross-subject or whole-book reliability.

## Impact

- Affected specifications: `book-pipeline`; new `factory-experimentation`.
- Likely affected implementation: `PROGRAM.md`, `loop/`, `scripts/loop/`,
  pipeline prompts, production-book templates, and the quit-sugar planning
  artifacts used by H-F01.
- Unchanged protections: `docs/BOOK-FACTORY-VISION.md`, method integrity,
  reference blindness during generation, source traceability, medical safety,
  originality gates, and the stable production-book paths.
- The retired calibration lab remains read-only archaeology.
- No factory behavior or product prose changes merely because this proposal
  exists; implementation follows the reviewed ledger in `tasks.md`.
