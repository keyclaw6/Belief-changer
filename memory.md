# Research-first factory mission

## Outcome and acceptance

Make the prompt-only research stage the smallest executable Windows-safe,
resumable, bounded, fail-closed research factory. It must plan coverage, run
five parallel discovery lanes, retain only fetched rights/privacy-eligible
sources, deduplicate and grade bounded evidence, derive belief/persona/safety/
slot adequacy, synthesize source-traceable LEU/SEU units, recursively fill only
demonstrated gaps, obtain independent evidence judgment, and seal one accepted
whole-book corpus. Framing/planning/commissioning/writing require that current
seal; every writer dispatch also requires its exact accepted chapter packet.
Research is a separate causal surface whose control/treatment research is
isolated, hard-gated, blindly judged, optionally tested on paired downstream
outcomes, and never confounded with writing changes.

Acceptance requires strict OpenSpec, focused deterministic and end-to-end
failure/pass/resume/Windows tests, captured no-network provider transport,
isolated native-Codex temporary simulations, two reviewed plan perspectives,
independent implementation and final acceptance review, canonical
`bash scripts/check.sh`, and reviewed commit/push to clean synchronized `main`.

## Implemented integrity decisions

- The fixed production route uses DeepSeek V4 Pro through bounded OpenRouter
  Responses search/fetch tools only after explicit start. Retention requires a
  completed matching fetch; search citations/model memory never count. Rights
  and privacy precede persistence; late rejection durably purges content.
- Eligible URL/content/excerpt/exact-evidence/semantic/story/study duplicates
  collapse before IDs or coverage. Scientific packet/item lineage is consistent,
  normalized case/whitespace cannot fake independence, and corroboration never
  inflates adequacy. Resumed provider/editor results are fully revalidated
  against request/task, reservation, route/model, usage, fetch, canonical
  payload, and verdict—not trusted from a recomputed outer hash.
- One shared validator derives ten-bank, floor, persona, belief-persona, slot,
  counter-corpus, safety, diversity, scientific-lineage, grade/inference, and
  LEU/SEU/GAP coverage from selected retained evidence. Independent rejection
  returns targeted gaps; rejection evidence accumulates across resumed rounds.
  Scarcity can waive only a numeric floor with bound attempt/editor evidence.
- A current whole-book seal gates framing, planning, commissioning, and writing.
  The immediate writer gate receives only exact assigned accepted units/locators.
  One audited writer batch pins the exact commission receipt; each later chapter
  rechecks that receipt plus the current seal/assigned needs. Expected preceding
  chapter outputs do not stale the pin, but any authority/research mutation does.
  A targeted extension is authorized only by a current independent plan review
  before commission audit or a current audit proving `unit_missing`; stale or
  tampered plan/audit/card bytes block. It preserves prior packet/unit bytes,
  reseals, and stales downstream bindings.
- Research experiments use two RF-02 arms from one accepted baseline. Physical
  control research prompt/editor/config bytes are rechecked before dispatch and
  at the final gate. Frozen identities cover process variables, not generated
  downstream outcomes. The blind research and downstream judges return only
  `A`/`B`/`TIE` plus a reason; deterministic code maps that to outcome afterward.
  Required downstream evidence is an exact blind comparison of both sealed,
  hard-gated arm outputs, never a one-arm product PASS. Promotion requires an
  exact named-human receipt bound to pair, record, evidence, and both seals.
- Read-only preflight/baseline/completed-run inspection accepts CANDIDATE or
  SEALED causal arms. Every research execution/mutation route remains
  CANDIDATE-only and rejects a sealed arm before transport/editor construction.
