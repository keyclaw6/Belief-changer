# AUTHORITATIVE SEMANTIC COMMISSION — C-01

C-01, working title *The Debate Can End*, owns the book’s entry contract. Its single belief move is that reading need not begin another diet, force an immediate decision, or demand proof of discipline. The reader is being invited to investigate whether the recurring sugar debate can end. The promised destination is desire-level freedom, not flawless control.

The chapter receives one skeptical, tired adult who has already tried diets, moderation rules, private bargaining, lapses, and restarts. Give particular recognition to the reader whose management plan has brought genuine relief but remains permanently on duty (R-05/RV-09), and the reader who no longer wants a perfect streak but wants the decision itself to stop recurring (R-06/RV-11). These are facets of the same reader, not separate audiences.

Make the central burden concrete as the decision tax: the attention consumed by anticipating, scanning, budgeting, postponing, negotiating, compensating, and resetting. The desired freedom is an ordinary absence of active debate. Distinguish it from both suppression, where behavior changes while the imagined reward remains precious, and managed truce, where rules and resets can provide real relief while continuing to require maintenance. Do not belittle managed truce or imply that choosing it is illegitimate. The chapter may make a quieter possibility visible without commanding the reader to pursue it.

Stay at D0/F1. This is trust, orientation, and permission—not benefit demolition. C-01 raises an honest question about BAD SUGAR’s claimed value but does not answer it, explain a mechanism, define a trap, or ask the reader to stop. Pleasant taste remains real, choice remains intact, and nothing about opening the book proves incapacity or powerlessness.

EV-06 and EV-08 are the only assigned evidence:

- EV-06 is one commercial wellness testimonial in which genuine liberation language coexists with more than fifteen years of daily practices, boundaries, exceptions, and resets. It permits the narrow point that real relief and substantial continuing management can coexist. It does not establish program efficacy, absence of desire, a universal trigger list, or the inferiority or falseness of management.
- EV-08 is one author’s self-report, not a trial. Its relevant meaning here is that the distinction between not eating dessert and no longer missing it is possible, and that recurring dessert-decision time diminished in this one account as desserts left the person’s active decision catalog. It supplies no probability, causal explanation, general efficacy claim, promise of ease, or assurance that the reader will reproduce the outcome. Do not import its reported physical, mental, holiday, or taste effects into this chapter’s promise.

SC-01 should make recurring dessert-decision labor recognizable without converting one person’s experience into a prediction. SC-08 should show that a practiced exception-and-reset system may be genuinely helpful while still having work to perform. The lived material must honor effort and relief; it must not become a testimonial composite or a disguised success claim.

The following frozen language must debut unchanged:

- M-01: `You can test whether BAD SUGAR owns any special or irreplaceable pleasure, without denying pleasant taste or giving up your right to choose.`
- M-02: `without deprivation, without battle, and without a lifetime of rules`
- M-06: `anticipating, bargaining, and starting over`

M-01 is the autonomy and honesty contract. M-02 distinguishes the intended outcome from suppression and perpetual management without promising that the book has already proved its case. M-06 names the recurring labor from which freedom is being offered.

C-01 owns one instruction, which must appear verbatim:

`READ WITH CURIOSITY, NOT DREAD; UNTIL YOU REACH YOUR CHOSEN THRESHOLD, EAT AS YOU NORMALLY DO, BEGIN NO NEW RESTRICTION OR COMPENSATION PLAN FOR THIS METHOD, AND KEEP FOLLOWING ANY QUALIFIED CLINICAL INSTRUCTION.`

I-01 governs only the pre-threshold reading period. It is permission to investigate without initiating a new restriction, compensation scheme, ceremonial final dose, or binge. It must not be framed as a permanent instruction or as permission to disregard existing care. Qualified clinical instructions outrank the method and remain outside its target.

Trust must come from exact recognition, bounded evidence, and an openly testable inquiry. State plainly that the book offers an investigation rather than authority credentials. Do not invent or imply an author-origin story, clinical pedigree, method success rate, scale-of-success dossier, testimonial consensus, or guaranteed result.

The reader must never be shamed, diagnosed, moralized at, or ordered to stop. Past difficulty is not evidence of weak character. Do not use fear, health harm, weight loss, metabolic repair, mental clarity, improved energy, taste change, craving timelines, addiction claims, or commercial-industry arguments to secure participation. Do not personify craving or install a replacement diet, purity identity, trigger-avoidance system, or permanent monitoring practice. Freedom should feel calm and ordinary, not heroic, superior, or transformative.

The exact BAD SUGAR boundary is intentionally not C-01’s work. Use the label only as necessary for the frozen promise and the recurring decision problem; do not adjudicate fruit, ordinary carbohydrates, ingredients, gray foods, accidents, or exceptions. Likewise, do not debut later trap, deprivation, mechanism, threshold, or lapse doctrine. The chapter’s handoff is simply a willing investigator who has begun no new restriction, understands that I-01 is temporary, and is ready for the target line to be drawn next.

Target length: approximately 2,800 words.
