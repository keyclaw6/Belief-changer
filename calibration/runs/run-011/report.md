FULL additionally received the unchanged complete 639-line style guide; NULL received no style guide. Neither saw `chapter-writer.md`, the master plan, previous prose, reviews, history, metrics, or reference material. Both drafts froze before objective measurement and before two independent, neutralized Sol-ultra reviews.

## Blind semantic results

The reviewers saw the canonical style guide, canonical plan, reviewer contract, and one neutral candidate. They never saw arm identity, writer inputs, the other candidate, history, hypothesis, metrics, or reference text. Arm mapping was opened only after both reviews completed.

FULL invented a gender, stopped binges, community, decision protocols, and program efficacy for EV-06, despite the commission permitting only the coexistence of reported relief and continuing management. It also converted EV-08 into “a man I know” with invented dinner-routine details, expanding material reserved for later work.

NULL independently changed one commercial testimonial into “A person I encountered” and asserted “Her system is working.” The frozen commission explicitly says EV-06 does not establish program efficacy. These are confidently traceable target blockers that were generated without style-guide exposure. NULL also omitted required anatomy and carried serious craft defects.

## Causal verdict — H-048 INCONCLUSIVE

### Raw outcome

The intended lever was instantiated exactly. Both arms had target ownership blockers. NULL's blocker is direct raw evidence that the full style guide is not necessary for Opus to invent an evidence relationship and efficacy claim. FULL's broader leakage remains compatible with the guide amplifying the defect.

### Why the formal verdict cannot be REFUTED

The preregistered sensitivity floor said an incomplete or under-2,380-word NULL draft is INCONCLUSIVE. NULL stopped normally at 2,229 words. Changing that rule after seeing a blocker would make the causal record outcome-dependent, so H-048 remains INCONCLUSIVE even though its raw signal points toward Opus prior sufficiency.

### Failure autopsy

The experiment removed `chapter-writer.md` from both arms because it repeats the suspect worldview and would have been a third semantic source. That made the style ablation clean, but it also removed the normal production writing contract. The commission's approximate 2,800-word target did not secure the production floor: FULL stopped at 2,081 and NULL at 2,229. The lever was instantiated, but the required exposure sensitivity was not.

This is not evidence for a retry with a better sample. An identical retry would be resampling, and hand-authoring a style capsule or length patch would introduce a new prompt confound. The experiment also cannot support removing the style guide: NULL failed anatomy, evidence fidelity, length, and craft.

### Rival explanations and signal risks

- Opus's persuasive prior may independently turn bounded lived material into a familiar success narrative.
- The commission's juxtaposition of relief and continuing management may invite embellishment even while its prohibitions are explicit.
- Full style exposure may amplify the breadth of leakage without being necessary for it.
- Both semantic reviews are from the same Sol family, although the deciding evidence claims are directly checkable against quoted commission boundaries.
- One under-floor specimen cannot rank these mechanisms or establish a universal production remedy.

## Product and factory result

Nothing is promoted. Run-011 changes no prompt and accepts no chapter. The pre-existing live Chapters 1–2 remain the last accepted product artifacts. Before another experiment, a fresh direction audit must choose one behavior-agnostic production-level question; there will be no length waiver, identical retry, Chapter 1 patch, packet resample, or handcrafted style capsule.

## Gate verdict

INCONCLUSIVE. The diagnostic produced useful raw evidence but failed its preregistered exposure condition. No Stage-A pairwise panel ran because neither draft was accepted product prose.
