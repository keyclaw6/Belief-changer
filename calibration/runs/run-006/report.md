# Run report — run-006

**Stage:** A **Scope:** Chapter 3 revision-mode A/B **Verdict:** IN PROGRESS

## What will run

Run-006 freezes the exact rejected Chapter 3 R3 artifact and its exact fresh review. Two Opus 4.6 reasoning-none arms receive the same full semantic context, review, craft objective, route, and endpoint maximum. Control A may rewrite the complete artifact; treatment B acts as a defect-scoped editor that preserves unaffected meaning while repairing the cited defects and measured craft surface. Both outputs complete before any review is opened.

Each arm then receives independent blind reviews from native GPT-5.6 Sol at "ultra" and OpenRouter Gemini 3.1 Pro at "high". The panel is a symmetric measurement instrument, not a second production lever. Reviewers may take as much time and reasoning as needed.

## Pre-registered causal test

- **Observation:** successive whole-artifact revisions repaired named defects but exposed or created different ones; claims present from R1 survived multiple reviews, while R3 introduced a new exact duplicate and amplified latent unsupported claims.
- **Mechanism:** whole-artifact resampling mutates already-correct prose when the actual repair is local; preserving unaffected meaning should lower regression risk.
- **One lever:** revision scope only — unconstrained whole rewrite versus defect-scoped edit.
- **Held constant:** exact start artifact and review; writer model, route, reasoning and output allowance; prompt/style/plan/continuity context; objective; blindness; reviewer panel and gate.
- **Prediction:** B removes all three blockers, stays inside the 2,210–2,990-word plan range, moves direct address toward 25–33/1,000 and short sentences materially toward ~20%, introduces no blocker, and receives convergent "ACCEPT"; A introduces a new semantic/repetition/evidence regression or loses the blind quality preference.
- **Decision:** support H-043 only if B convergently accepts and A does not. Refute only if A convergently accepts while B fails because preservation prevented a necessary repair. Matching outcomes or material reviewer disagreement are inconclusive.
- **Product rule:** a sole convergently accepted arm may become live Chapter 3. If both accept, a fresh blind pairwise preference selects the product while revision-scope causality remains inconclusive.

## Factory-generalization boundary

This experiment changes no shared factory asset. Sugar and Chapter 3 supply the frozen test specimen only. Even a supported result is not a factory rule until it replicates on another chapter and ultimately passes the untouched caffeine holdout with zero amendments.

## Gate verdict

FAIL. Neither arm received the preregistered convergent "ACCEPT", so neither becomes live Chapter 3.

H-043 is **INCONCLUSIVE** by its preregistered rule: both arms had matching non-convergent outcomes and each reviewer pair materially disagreed. The scoped arm did not meet its absolute quality prediction, but the control failed on substantially the same defects; this run therefore cannot attribute the failure to revision scope or claim that whole resampling was better.

## Failure autopsy

### Raw outcome

Both writing modes repaired the three defects explicitly named in the R3 review, yet both retained a broader claim that a story causes the felt pull and dissolves it when challenged. Both also generalized illustrative childhood, advertising, cultural, or placement scenes beyond the evidence assigned to C-03. The scoped arm preserved those latent claims; the whole-rewrite arm regenerated close equivalents.

### Ownership audit

The accepted plan is not contradictory on this boundary. C-03 assigns EV-01, EV-02, and EV-11, whose permitted inferences are reported reasons, assigned jobs, and felt language—not a developmental or universal mechanism. Its guardrail explicitly forbids saying advertising created every belief. C-10 alone owns the anticipation–closure mechanism and says its learning evidence is mixed; C-13 owns bounded commercial optimization.

The immediate product failure is therefore writer noncompliance with available generic context, compounded by incomplete prior review detection. The cross-family measurement also exposed a separate reviewer-reliability problem: Gemini praised both chapters as technically flawless while mismeasuring basic artifact facts and missing the evidence/job boundary that Sol independently identified in both.

### Causal interpretation and signal risk

Sol may be applying the plan boundary more conservatively than a general reader would, and some installation language can function rhetorically rather than as a scientific claim. Gemini's independent agreement on acceptance is therefore not discarded. However, method integrity ranks above polish, and Gemini's large metric errors make its blanket technical assurance insufficient to override specific, plan-cited blockers.

Calling scoped revision supported because it repaired the old defects would be a false positive: it missed the preregistered craft target and did not converge. Calling it refuted would also be false: the control failed on the same boundary, so preservation was not shown to be the cause. No shared factory asset or Chapter 3 prose is promoted from this result.
