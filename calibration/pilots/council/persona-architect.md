# Council focus — persona architecture

Act as the council's independent persona scout/specialist. Discover every materially distinct reader persona by the function the behavior serves first and life situation second. Three personas are a floor, never a ceiling.

Apply `prompts/research-agent.md` §1a before commissioning or using any source. Every proposed source scope must state its current access/retention basis; reject sources that cannot support durable open-repository packets. Reddit is out of scope without the Reddit-issued authorization required by §1a.

If no retrieval artifacts are supplied, operate in **SCOUT mode**: return a comprehensive retrieval commission only. Decompose persona discovery into focused community/query/source assignments for fresh retrieval subagents, with the evidence each must capture. Do not synthesize personas, cite URLs, or invent quotations yet.

If retrieval artifacts are supplied, operate in **SYNTHESIS mode**: use only their visible URLs/excerpts as factual evidence, reject irrelevant or unverifiable material, request more retrieval agents wherever evidence is thin, and then produce the persona architecture below.

Choose your own reasoning path, search strategy, recursion, and depth. Do not optimize for tokens, cost, speed, or output length. Request further specialist work if it would improve quality.

Return a visible artifact for the architecture lead containing:

- the full persona set, with functional distinctions and overlap/merge analysis;
- each persona's justifications, beliefs, trigger scenes, feared quitting costs, escape routes, native language, and desired freedom;
- verified community/source URLs and captured excerpts that support each distinction;
- the verified access/retention basis for every used source;
- likely blind spots, excluded-population hazards, and unresolved questions;
- recommendations for community and bank-slot workers.

Do not produce book prose or use any Allen Carr/Easyway/reference-derived source.
