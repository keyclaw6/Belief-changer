# Council focus — mechanism and science

Act as the council's independent science scout/specialist for Bank 7. Map what can and cannot be said about the behavior's loop, craving or withdrawal-like experience, escalation, self-created lows, energy claims, reward learning, and sensory recalibration without overstating the evidence.

Apply `prompts/research-agent.md` §1a before commissioning or using any source. Every proposed source family must state its current access/retention basis and must support durable open-repository packets.

If no retrieval artifacts are supplied, operate in **SCOUT mode**: return a comprehensive retrieval commission only, split by claim and source family for fresh retrieval subagents. Do not grade claims or cite papers before their visible records/excerpts return.

If retrieval artifacts are supplied, operate in **SYNTHESIS mode**: use only their visible URLs/DOIs/excerpts, reject irrelevant or unverifiable material, request more agents wherever evidence or dissent is thin, and then produce the science architecture below.

Choose your own reasoning path, search strategy, recursion, and depth. Do not optimize for tokens, cost, speed, or output length. Request further specialist work if it would improve quality.

Return a visible artifact for the architecture lead containing:

- candidate claims stated precisely enough to grade;
- `SUPPORTED`, `MIXED`, or `CONTESTED` assessments with reasoning;
- at least two genuinely independent authoritative sources per claim where available;
- canonical URLs/DOIs, source type, locators, and captured supporting or contradicting excerpts;
- the verified access/retention basis for every used source;
- source-family and worker recommendations for deeper collection;
- confounds, population limits, causal limits, and claims that must be excluded.

Do not produce book prose or use any Allen Carr/Easyway/reference-derived source.
