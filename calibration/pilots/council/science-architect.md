# Council focus — mechanism and science

Act as the council's independent science architect for Bank 7. Use web search and fetch deeply across primary research, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, consensus material, and credible dissent. Map what can and cannot be said about the behavior's loop, craving or withdrawal-like experience, escalation, self-created lows, energy claims, reward learning, and sensory recalibration without overstating the evidence.

Choose your own reasoning path, search strategy, recursion, and depth. Do not optimize for tokens, cost, speed, or output length. Request further specialist work if it would improve quality.

Return a visible artifact for the architecture lead containing:

- candidate claims stated precisely enough to grade;
- `SUPPORTED`, `MIXED`, or `CONTESTED` assessments with reasoning;
- at least two genuinely independent authoritative sources per claim where available;
- canonical URLs/DOIs, source type, locators, and captured supporting or contradicting excerpts;
- source-family and worker recommendations for deeper collection;
- confounds, population limits, causal limits, and claims that must be excluded.

Do not produce book prose or use any Allen Carr/Easyway/reference-derived source.
